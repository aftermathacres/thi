About

THI (Technical Innovations, formerly TecHome Innovations) is a licensed, bonded, and insured California contractor specializing in commercial audio, video, and IT integration.

Registered Veteran Owned Business. Proudly supporting our community and fellow veterans through service and leadership.

Since 1991, we've evolved from custom installations to becoming the trusted partner for enterprise facilities across Northern California. Our focus is simple: complex installations executed with exceptional technical skill and a smooth, professional approach.

Led by founder Ivan Brownell, our team brings decades of experience in pro audio, AV integration, and IT infrastructure. We design custom solutions that fit your needs—whether it's a conference room upgrade or a complete facility buildout.
